🤖 AI Impact on Jobs — Analytics Dashboard (2010–2025)
A data analytics project exploring how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping the global job market across industries from 2010 to 2025.

📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a 5,000-row dataset sourced from Kaggle to uncover trends in AI-driven job displacement, automation risk, reskilling demand, and job posting growth across 10 major industries.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn) — data cleaning & visualization
Power BI — interactive dashboard
GitHub — version control


📂 Dataset

Source: Kaggle — AI Impact on Jobs (2010–2025)
Size: 5,000 rows
Key columns: Industry, Salary, Automation Risk Score, AI Adoption Stage, Reskilling Required, Displacement Risk, Posting Year


🔍 Key Findings

Healthcare and Energy show highest automation risk (above 0.60) — most vulnerable to AI displacement
Finance and Tech have lowest risk — AI is augmenting roles, not replacing them
Job postings peaked around 2020–2022, reflecting post-pandemic demand for AI-skilled professionals
AI displacement risk is nearly equal — 34% High, 34% Medium, 32% Low — widespread impact
Reskilling demand is highest in Manufacturing, Healthcare, and Retail
